## 🚀 Getting started

When you open TRACE for the first time, you will see a dashboard. This space shows your current tasks and active projects. 

### Adding a new task
To create a task, click the "New Task" button in the top left corner. Enter the task name and a short description. You can set a due date to keep yourself on schedule. The system saves this information locally on your computer.

### Tracking your time
TRACE uses a built-in timer for every task. You can start the timer when you begin working and stop it when you take a break or finish the work. The application calculates the total time spent on each item automatically. This history helps you understand how long complex projects take to complete.

### Reviewing your history
The "History" tab displays all your past tasks. You can filter these by date or category. Use this view to see what you completed last week or last month. The system preserves these records so you can review your growth over time.

## 💡 Core features

* Offline-first design: All your data stays on your machine. You do not need an internet connection to use the software.
* Task accountability: The system tracks every change you make to a task. This creates a clear timeline of your work.
* Progress logs: You can add notes to any task. Use these to capture lessons or ideas while you work.
* Simple interface: The layout focuses on functionality to reduce distractions.
* Data security: Since the files remain on your local hard drive, you maintain full control over your information. 

## 🛡️ Privacy and data

TRACE stores all information in a local file on your computer. The application does not send your task data to any remote servers. You are the only person with access to your history and progress logs. If you wish to back up your data, you can copy the TRACE data folder to an external hard drive or a cloud storage service of your choice.
